ABOUT THE VOLTA FASHION AWARDS

Volta fashion awards is an awards set to appreciate, celebrate and promote fashion designers, fashion brands, fashion personalities and other individuals in the fashion industry in the Volta Region. The award comes with categories that will discover fashion industry players in every part of Volta region and give them a sensitive platform to sprint to the national level. The awards come with contracts and plaques to various winners from each category. th The maiden edition was held on 19 October, 2019 at the Ho Technical University JCR and it was such a great event. The organizers of this event want to sustain it as they want it to be a yearly event to help promote the fashion industry in the Volta Region. Due to this, work towards the 2020 edition begun early 2020 but had to be called off due to the Corona Virus Pandemic. Work towards the 2021 edition which is dabbed the “Royal Edition” is in play. The organizes intend to make it a very memorable and classic event hence the name Royal Edition.
